the shape of it (rough)

this page isn't finished yet — but the work is. coaching is private, ongoing, and tuned to your practice. most people come in with one specific question about posture or physical discomfort in sitting, and we work from there.

i'll have proper details up soon. in the meantime, the fastest way to get a real answer is to write me.

↳ you can always ask first, decide later.

who it tends to help

people who meditate seriously and are hitting a specific physical wall — knees, lower back, shoulders, a sit that used to feel fine and doesn't anymore.

people who want one person to think carefully about their body in the context of their practice, rather than doing the thinking alone.

what it is, roughly

1:1, remote, on zoom
usually weekly or biweekly
paced to your practice, not a program
we start with a free call to see if it fits
